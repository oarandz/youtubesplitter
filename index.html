Video Wall

multiview gallery · keyboard mixer · live feeds
Video accepts watch?v=, youtu.be/, /live/, /embed/, /shorts/ or bare IDs. All RSS feeds are merged into one wire, tagged by source, newest first, refreshing every 30 seconds. New headlines flash orange for 30 seconds; anything timestamped 5 minutes ago or less flashes red for the whole 5 minutes. Feeds are fetched through public CORS proxies (one per feed to spread the load) — if the wire stays on "failed", the proxies are likely rate-limited or a URL isn't a valid feed.
